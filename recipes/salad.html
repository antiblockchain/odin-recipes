Salad

Want to stay healthy? Try this easy and quick recipe.

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups torn leaf lettuce
  • 1 hard-boiled large egg, chopped
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon cider vinegar
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper

    Steps

  1. In a salad bowl, combine the lettuce, egg and onion. In a small bowl, whisk the mayonnaise, vinegar and pepper. Pour over salad and toss to coat.
