Club sandwich

This is a very easy meal for anyone who doesn't have time for a huge meal but still wants something delicious to eat.

    Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • Bacon
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayo

    Steps

  1. First Layer: Spread the mayo on one side of lightly toasted bread. Add turkey, tomato and cheese (be sure to put the tomatoes in the middle so the bread doesnâ€™t get soggy).
  2. Second Layer: Layer ham, bacon and lettuce.
  3. Top with the final slices of bread, secure with toothpicks and cut into quarters.
