Homemade pizza

This recipe is a quick and easy pizza that anyone can make at home with a little bit of time and effort.

    Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp yeast
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp oil

    • Toppings

  • You may use whatever toppings you would like. Preferably use pizza or tomato sauce, and mozerella cheese.

    Steps

  1. In large bowl, mix first 4 ingredients.
  2. Mix water and oil; add to flour mixture.
  3. Turn onto floured surface; knead for 2 minutes.
  4. Place in a greased bowl; turning to grease top.
  5. Cover and let rise for 20 minutes.
  6. Punch down; place on 12in, greased pizza pan.
  7. Pat into a circle.
  8. Cover in sauce and toppings of choice
  9. Bake at 400* for 20 minutes or until light brown.
